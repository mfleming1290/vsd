About Us

The Veterans Service Directory is a listing of local businesses who offer special incenties to active and retired members of the armed services. These special incentives include but are not limited to Veterans Discounts, Veteran employment opportunities, special programs for Veterans like the VA Home loan program, and Veteran education progarms. Our service directories are available to Veterans through our website and inside of many different Veteran social organizations like the VFW, The American Legion, The DAV, Elks Lodges and other locations. For a list of our locations near you please contact us.

Employment

Our Employment directory is a network of businesses nationwide that hire Veterans, and provide skills training for active and retired members of the armed services. Please visit the directory and feel free to contact these businesses if you are a Veteran looking for employment.

Benefits

  1. Our guides not only connect Veterans with military programs but also with local businesses who offer special services and discounts for past and present members of the armed services.

  2. Veterans Post receive a percentage of money raised through sponsors as well as a digital billboard and resource guide at no cost.

  3. Through this program local businesses are able to show their support in the community for those who have served this country with honor. They are also able to promote their own services to the most loyal group of men and women in this nation.
